# Product

## Register
product

## Users
Investors, crypto enthusiasts, and project creators on a Web3 crowdfunding platform. They are data-driven, looking for promising projects, clear roadmaps, and funding status.

## Product Purpose
A high-end, cyberpunk-themed Web3 crowdfunding platform (RadiantVoid). It provides detailed project pages, interactive data logs, and funding tracking.

## Brand Personality
Cyberpunk, Neon, Futuristic, High-tech, Professional.

## Anti-references
- Clean, minimalist flat SaaS (e.g., Notion, Stripe clones).
- Pastel or light/bland themes.
- Generic corporate dashboards.

## Design Principles
1. Immersive Neon: Deep dark backgrounds with deliberate, vibrant neon accents.
2. Clear Hierarchy: High contrast in typography scale and weight to guide the eye through dense information.
3. Purposeful Motion: Smooth exponential transitions, no bounce or elasticity.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Maintain readable contrast for body text against dark backgrounds. Keep neon glows subtle behind text to avoid eye strain.
